Design Parameters

When creating this phone, we wanted to make a phone which was revolutionary in its own right. To achieve this, we created a self-healing back of the phone which will allow it to recover and repair small cuts, scratches, and cracks. We decided that we also wanted to add all the latest features to this phone, so we decked it out with the latest specifications to ever land on a phone.

I carefully considered the materials of the phone, as well as the design. I really liked the design of the Samsung Galaxy S21 Ultra, and I took inspiration from that because I like the Camera glass curving into the side rails of the phone. With the glass covering the front of the phone, I wanted to give you great protection for everyday activities so I reinforced the front glass of this phone with Gorilla Glass Victus 2 which was a glass compound introduced in 2023. With the side rails of the phone, I have equipped the phone with the very best Armour Aluminum to protect the sides of the phone from damage from drops.

Specifications

  • Screen Size: 6.5" OLED (3020x1440)
  • Processor: Snapdragon 8 Gen 1
  • RAM: 12GB (LPDDR5)
  • Storage: 128GB / 256GB (UFS 3.1)
  • Battery: 5000 mAh (7:50 Hrs:Mins)
  • Refresh Rate: 10-144Hz
  • Operating System: Android 12 (Up to Android 15)
  • IP Rating: IP68
  • Physical Size and Weight: 7 Inches and 225 Grams
  • Camera: Main Camera 48 MP f/1.9 (OIS), Telephoto Lens 3.0X 24 MP f/2.4 (OIS), Ultrawide Lens 0.5X 12 MP f/1.5

Connectivity

  • Mobile Network Technology: 5G (frequency bands 700MHz, 850MHz, 2600MHz)/4G LTE/3G/ VoLTE
  • Wi-Fi 6E (Version 802.11ax)
  • Bluetooth 5.3
  • USB-C
  • NFC
  • UWB

Features

  • Self-Healing finish on the back of the phone, which will make it the first ever phone to survive everyday life actions, from scratches to drops from pocket height.
  • Quad speaker setup for virtual surround sound capabilities, to immerse yourself in your content like never before
  • 4K AMOLED Screen for unparalleled quality, with a 144 Hz Screen Refresh Rate, so that you feel that your content is alive, and you can see every detail in your screen like it was meant to be.
